Rules:

Objective of this game is to guess the hidden combination of symbols.

If the player can't guess the hidden combination the game is lost and it will be revealed with the message. Otherwise the game is won.

Do note that these are combinations with repeating. Which means each symbol can be repeated in combination maximum 4 times. Therefore the total number of combinations is 4096.

How is it played:

1. The player enters a combination in the designated area. The player can delete symbols from his/hers current combination with a press of a button.

2. Once the player eneters a 4 symbols hits the Go button, the combination is being listed in list of all combinations and it is compared against the hidden combination.

3. Depending on the result of this comparison, this list beside combinations list is populated with appropriet colors. Red for direct hit, yellow for inderict hit (which means the symbol is in the hidden combination but it is at wrong spot) and gray for a miss.

4. The player repeats this process maximum 5 times.

And that is it. Enjoy the game.
